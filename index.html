Go-to-Market
& Sales Strategy

Workshop-based, 2–4 weeks, remote or on-site

Your product works. But leads aren’t coming in consistently, your sales team operates without a clear system, or you’re losing deals you should be winning.

I build a sales strategy with you that fits your stage: Who are your best customers, how do you reach them, what’s your story, and what does a sales engine look like that grows predictably? Whether seed stage or €20M revenue, whether hardware, SaaS, or services.

Sparring &
Advisory

Focused sessions, remote or on-site

For founders and commercial leaders who need an experienced sparring partner to challenge assumptions, sharpen priorities, and bring clarity to growth decisions.

We work together on the real questions: where to focus, what to fix first, how to improve commercial direction, and what actions will create momentum fastest.
